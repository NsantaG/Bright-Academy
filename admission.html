Admission Procedure

Thanks for your enquiries. This is the admissions procedure:

1. Pick up a Registration Form ₦10,000 for Nursery and Primary schools from the Admissions Office.

2. Return completed form with:

  • 2 Passport Photographs
  • Photocopy of Immunization Record
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • Transcript or Photocopies of past school results for three previous & consecutive years if the child has been in school that long.
NB: Both parents are required to sign the application form and attend the interview.

3. Schedules an assessment date with the Head of Nursery/Key stage.

4. Assessments for the primary school takes place in March. Other assessments take place all year round depending on available openings.

Online Admission

Applications will only be processed;

  • If a non refundable application fee of ₦10,000.00 (Ten Thousand Naira Only) has been paid into the school account and a scanned receipt of payment received by the school accounts department.
  • If all required documents have been received by the admissions officer.
